The CMS

As the digital age becomes increasingly complex, pervasive and multi-platform, I aim to give my clients the digital insight in order for them to know their online customers. I use Wordpress as my CMS of choice to solve problems and help clients add value. I've been hooked and knee deep in code ever since I first saw the power of Wordpress.

In its wake, cloud computing has introduced a suite of cloud technology and associated acronyms such as SaaS (Software as a service) and MBaaS (mobile backend as a service). Its implications are many, but for developers such as myself, this demonstrates the beauty and power of the internet. For my clients looking to enter the mobile space it means their mobile applications will enjoy added value from features such as:

  • Cross platform apps that support Apple iOS, Android, Blackberry and Windows phones.
  • Email notifications.
  • User Management facilities such as user registration, login, and password recovery.

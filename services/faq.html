4. How can Search Engine Optimization help my business?
Just as a map helps you find your destination, Search Engine Optimization (SEO) helps people find your website. I can improve your rankings with search engines. SEO increases your online visibility by reducing the number of clicks it takes to find you, which leads to increased sales.
5. What does the price include?
The prices cover the markup with the implementation of the following options: - Conversion of PSD files into default WordPress . - Installation of WordPress on your server.
6. What is included in WordPress by default?
The standard package includes the following options: - Styling of the theme in accordance with the designs. Pages that are not included in markup but present in the theme will be stylized at our own discretion. - implementation of the default CMS functionality .
7. What is involved in getting my design coded into a WordPress Theme?
You upload the files, fill out the form and let us know what you would like to have done. We investigate your request and calculate the final quote for your project. (Please note that additional custom features will cost extra.) We accept "upfront" payments only. Once we have coded your theme, we upload it to a demo server so you can have a look at what the finished product will be like. If there are any issues, they will be corrected free of charge; however, updates will be quoted separately.
8. How will my website be hosted?
There are 3 options :) a) I provide hosting for my clients on my server which means you wont have to worry about headaches related to hosting. b) If you want your own private hosting then I can setup your own server and upload the final work to your new server. At the end of the project, I hand you the keys and be on your way. c) If you already have a hosting somewhere else, I simply upload your new website to your server and jobs done.
